ScholarSync Workspace

A redesigned design system inspired by Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and Apple's productivity suite. Built for deep work, deep thinking, and free-flowing research.

Design Principles

🎨

Content First

The workspace disappears. Your writing surface, research results, and library command the stage. Chrome is minimal.

🧘

Calm Workspace

Warm stone tones, generous whitespace, and quiet typography. No glowing neon or SaaS dashboards. A desk, not a cockpit.

🏢

Workspace, Not Product

Each tool has its own identity color (like Google Docs/Sheets/Slides). Navigation is a thin rail, not a heavy sidebar.

Key Structural Changes from Current Design

Current (SaaS-style)

  • ❌ Dark slate backgrounds (#020617)
  • ❌ Glass morphism with blur effects
  • ❌ Neon glow accents
  • ❌ Heavy 264px sidebar
  • ❌ Brand indigo (#6366f1) everywhere
  • ❌ "Luminous" SaaS copy style
  • ❌ Dense, feature-packed layout

New (Workspace-style)

  • ✅ Warm stone canvas (#FAFAF9)
  • ✅ Clean surfaces, no blur
  • ✅ Quiet, purposeful color
  • ✅ 64px icon rail + contextual sidebar
  • ✅ Per-app identity colors
  • ✅ Calm, academic-tone copy
  • ✅ Generous whitespace, breathing room
