✨ AI Synthesis

Consensus

Strong evidence supports that SGLT-2 inhibitors reduce major adverse cardiovascular events in patients with type 2 diabetes, particularly those with established cardiovascular disease.

● Strong Consensus (47 papers)

Key Findings

EMPA-REG OUTCOME showed 38% reduction in cardiovascular death with empagliflozin (HR 0.62, 95% CI 0.49-0.77).
CANVAS demonstrated 14% reduction in MACE (HR 0.86, 95% CI 0.75-0.97), though individual components did not reach significance.
DECLARE-TIMI 58 showed benefit primarily in heart failure hospitalization (HR 0.73, 95% CI 0.61-0.88) rather than MACE.

Evidence Gaps

Limited data on long-term outcomes beyond 4 years. Underrepresentation of non-White populations. Few studies in patients without established CVD.
