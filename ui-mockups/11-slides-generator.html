CRISPR-Cas9:
Ethics & Efficacy

A Comparative Analysis of High-Fidelity Variants

Presented by Dr. Rahul Verma

Introduction

  • CRISPR technology has revolutionized gene editing since 2012.
  • Primary challenge: Off-target effects (unintended mutations).
  • This study compares wild-type Cas9 vs. SpCas9-HF1 variants.

Methodology

We utilized a sample size of 500 embryos across three experimental groups, with comprehensive genomic sequencing at 24h, 48h, and 72h post-transfection.
